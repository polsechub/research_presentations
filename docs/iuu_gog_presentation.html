Why are the Gulf of Guinea Waters so Dangerous? The Expected and Unintended Consequences of Conflict, Poverty, and Illegal Fishing

Piracy world-wide

Is illegal fishing related to maritime piracy?

  • Past works: illegal fishing and piracy correlated
  • Foreign trawlers–>Deplete fishery–>Local fishers turn to piracy (Ploch et al. 2011; Weir 2009; Desai and Shambaugh 2021)
  • But seemingly plausible, lacks evidence (Denton and Harris 2021; Jesperson 2022)
  • Our focus on the local-level illegal fishing
